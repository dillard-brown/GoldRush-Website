Our Story

[Owner Name] started Gold Rush Hauling because they saw a gap in the market.

Junk removal companies in Yolo and Napa County were either big national platforms with no local relationships, or small owner-operators without the capacity to handle serious cleanout work.

Three situations motivated the most: seeing UC Davis students struggle to clear apartments every June with nowhere to turn. Watching real estate agents scramble to find a reliable hauler before a listing goes live. And hearing from families who couldn't find anyone patient and respectful enough to handle a parent's lifetime of accumulated belongings.

Gold Rush Hauling was built to solve all three.

Our Commitment to the Environment

Landfill is
always the last resort.

Every load gets sorted before it reaches the landfill. We partner with local nonprofits to donate usable goods, recycle metal, electronics, and materials, and only send to the landfill what truly has nowhere else to go.

We track our diversion rates and publish them. Ask us what we've donated or recycled on your job — we'll tell you exactly.

Eco diversion reports available on every job, on request.
